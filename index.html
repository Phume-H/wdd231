About Me

Welcome to my course page! My name is Mpumelelo, but most people call me Phume. I’m proudly South African, born and raised in the vibrant city of Durban, where I live with my family. I’m currently a full-time online student at BYU–Idaho, studying Web and Software Development a field that beautifully blends my love for creativity with the power of logic and technology.

I’m a passionate sports enthusiast with a special love for soccer, and I’ve always been fascinated by cars — from their design and engineering to the thrill of performance on the road. Sports have taught me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance, while my interest in cars reflects my curiosity for innovation and detail. Beyond these personal passions, what truly defines me is my commitment to making a difference in people’s lives. I believe that energy and talent should be used not only for personal enjoyment but also as a way to uplift others. Helping the less fortunate is something I care deeply about. Whether it’s through community initiatives, volunteering, or simply offering support to those in need, I strive to inspire hope and create opportunities for others. I see every act of kindness as a step toward building stronger, more compassionate communities. For me, success is not just measured by goals scored on the field or milestones achieved in life, but by the positive impact I leave behind. My journey is guided by passion, empathy, and a desire to connect with people in meaningful ways, using my interests as fuel to bring joy and encouragement wherever I go.

I strive to live as a disciple of Jesus Christ, and I seek Him daily in everything I do. Romans 8:35–39 is a passage that speaks directly to the heart, offering assurance that God’s love is unbreakable no matter what we face. Paul lists trials like hardship, persecution, famine, and danger, yet boldly declares that none of these can separate us from the love of Christ. He goes even further, saying that neither death nor life, angels nor demons, the present nor the future, nor any powers in all creation can ever cut us off from God’s love revealed in Jesus. This is my favorite scripture that uplifts me. Thank you for visiting my page. I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and building a life and career that reflects creativity, service, and faith.
